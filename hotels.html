1. Hilton Colombo

Overlooking the Indian Ocean and Beira Lake, Hilton Colombo Hotel provides direct access to Colombo’s World Trade Centre. It features an outdoor pool, 24-hour business center and 10 dining outlets. Featuring scenic views from over-sized windows, spacious rooms at Colombo Hilton feature a spacious workspace and a comfortable sofa. They come with a marble bathroom featuring separate shower and bathtub areas. Enjoy the use of the hot tub after an active workout at the well-equipped fitness center or tennis courts. Alternatively, guests can relax with an award-winning cocktail by the outdoor pool bar. Dining highlights at Hilton Colombo includes local specialties at the open-air Curry Leaf restaurant, Sri Lanka's only trattoria, Il Ponte, features homemade pasta. Hilton Colombo Hotel is a 45-minute drive from the Bandaranaike International Airport. It's within walking distance of the shopping and dining options at Fort District and Pettah Bazaar.
